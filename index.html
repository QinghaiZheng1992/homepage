I am a Ph.D. candidate student majoring in computer science and technology from Xi'an Jiaotong University advised by Prof. Jihua Zhu. I received the bachelor degree and master degree from Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2015 and 2018.

My research interests include machine learning, data mining, multi-view learning and label distribtion learning.
