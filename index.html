Qinghai Zheng (郑清海)

Brief Biography

I'm Qinghai Zheng, I am a PhD candidate of Xi’an Jiaotong University advised by Professor Jihua Zhu. I received his B.S. degree and M.S. degree from Xi’an Jiaotong University in 2015 and 2018.

Research Interest

My research interests are machine learning and data mining, including multi-view learning, subspace clustering, and label distribution learning.
